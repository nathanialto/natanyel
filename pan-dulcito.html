Pan Dulcito Design

about

I created a visual concept design called Pan Dulcito. I built the brand around an illustration of a concha, a Mexican pastry.

colors and font

In the logo, I utilized the font Roca and the lead colors #e54e9a, #e59029, and #8e4f36 which are references to the darker shades their respective illustrations.

stickers

I also created a sticker design with a simpler version of the logo and text referencing the brand concept.
